Daily Water Intake Calculation

Calculation Method

Our app calculates recommended daily water intake using scientifically-based guidelines:

Base Calculation:
  • 30ml of water per kg of body weight (base need)
  • Adjusted based on activity level:
    • Sedentary: × 1.0
    • Lightly Active: × 1.1
    • Moderately Active: × 1.2
    • Very Active: × 1.3
    • Extra Active: × 1.4

Scientific References

1. European Food Safety Authority (EFSA)
  • Recommends 2.5L daily for men, 2.0L for women
  • Source: "Scientific Opinion on Dietary Reference Values for Water"
  • URL: EFSA Journal
2. Institute of Medicine (US)
  • Recommends about 3.7L for men, 2.7L for women
  • Source: "Dietary Reference Intakes for Water"
  • URL: National Academies Press
3. World Health Organization (WHO)
  • Guidelines for adequate daily water intake
  • Source: "Nutrients in Drinking Water"
  • URL: WHO Repository
